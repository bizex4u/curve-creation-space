We started this journal for one reason: most advertising content online is generic, written for nobody, optimised for search engines instead of operators. We wanted a place to publish the opposite — short, specific notes from the campaigns we actually run.

## What you'll find here

- **Campaign teardowns** — what we planned, what we shipped, what it cost, and what we'd do differently.
- **Channel deep-dives** — when outdoor beats digital, when cinema beats radio, and when barter beats cash.
- **Operator playbooks** — the spreadsheets, briefs and negotiation patterns we use internally.

## Who it's for

Founders, CMOs, and brand managers in India who already buy media and want sharper instincts about where every rupee goes. If you're choosing between a metro takeover and three months of programmatic, this blog is for you.

## A note on tone

We won't pad posts to hit a word count. Some entries will be 300 words. That's intentional — your time is worth more than our SEO.
